Polymarket × Kalshi · Wendy two-pointer matcher

Cross-venue arbitrage: positions & prices

For each market: the YES price on both venues, the net signed YES-equivalent position z built by the matched arbitrage flow, and the price-invariant locked-in cash ypoly + ykalshi.

Positions are cumulated over matched fills from arbitrage_final.csv in execution order; a NO purchase is booked as a YES short at the implied price (1 − no_price). Prices are the last trade in each time bucket of the merged tape (~700 buckets per market). Because every match is fully hedged, zpoly = −zkalshi throughout and the locked-in series never depends on where prices go next.
